Evangelion: 1.0 You Are (Not) Alone

Reimagining THE LEGENDARY STORY

The original Neon Genesis Evangelion became super popular for its exciting story, deep characters, emotional moments and deep themes. But years passed, and the creators decided it was time to retell the story for a new generation.

In 2007, Evangelion: 1.0 You Are (Not) Alone was released, bringing back the series with amazing animation, a faster story, and some small changes that showed something bigger might be coming. It's great for people who never saw the original and exciting for fans who loved it before.

A FRESH TAKE ON A CLASSIC STORY

While Evangelion: 1.0 follows the same basic story as the first few episodes of the original series, there are plenty of changes that make it feel fresh and new. The most obvious difference is the animation, it's brighter, smoother, and packed with stunning modern visuals that make every battle and emotional moment even more intense.

The pacing is faster, too. Some scenes are streamlined or changed slightly, which keeps the story moving quickly while still capturing all the key moments fans loved in the original. There are also small hints of new ideas sprinkled throughout, teasing that the Rebuild series might be heading in a different direction later on. Even longtime fans will notice tiny details that feel fresh or surprising.
